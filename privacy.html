Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

OpsPilot ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our operations audit tool at opspilot.com.

Information We Collect

When you complete our free operations audit, we collect:

  • Your work email address
  • Responses to our audit questionnaire (revenue range, operational bottlenecks, software stack)
  • Automatically generated audit results (efficiency score, savings estimates)

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your personalized operations audit report via email
  • To sync your information with our CRM for follow-up communications you have opted into
  • To improve our audit tool and service offerings
  • To comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — website hosting and serverless infrastructure
  • Google Analytics — anonymized website usage analytics
  • HubSpot / Make.com / Zapier — CRM and lead management (if configured)

Data Retention

We retain audit submission data for up to 24 months, or until you request deletion, whichever comes first.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@opspilot.com.

Cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can opt out via your browser settings or the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@opspilot.com.
